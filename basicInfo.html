Basic Info

  • Title
  • Romeo and Juliet
  • Author
  • William Shakespeare
  • Published
  • London, England. Thomas Creede, 1597.
  • Genre
  • Play, tragedy, romance.
  • Setting
  • The play is set during the Renaissance in the cities of Verona and Mantua of northern Italy.
  • Point Of View
  • Though it is a play it does seem to follow the point of view of Romeo and Juliet, the two protagonists. However it does also follow the point of view of other servants of Montague and Capulet.
  • Conflict
  • The major conflict in the play deals with the love felt between Romeo and Juliet. The two lovers come from warring families and their love is forbidden. They must do all they can to keep the love secret, however due to the trouble this causes a tragic fate awaits them.
