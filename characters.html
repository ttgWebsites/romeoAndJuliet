Characters

  • Romeo
  • Son and heir of the Montague family
  • He's young about 16, he is very passionate and impulsive
  • Living in the middle of a conflict between his family's and the Capulet's, his only interest is love
  • Falls in love with Juliet, a Capulet, and secretly marries her, ultimately costs him his life
  • Juliet
  • Young girl of about 14, is the daughter of the Capulet family
  • She is also very passionate and driven by love much like Romeo
  • Falls in love quickly and marries Romeo despite her families conflict, and ultimately leads to her death
  • Friar Lawrence
  • Friend to Romeo and Juliet, secretly marries the couple hoping to bring peace to Verona
  • The Nurse
  • She is Juliet's nurse and has partially raised her since a young age
  • She is very faithful to Juliet, helping her at first with her relationship with Romeo, however in the end has an argument with Juliet over Juliet's passionate romance
  • Tybalt
  • He is a Capulet, and is very vain, and desires respect, often willing to challenge people he feels insult him
  • He is a strong swordsman and despises the Montagues
  • Capulet
  • The leader of the Capulet family, and Juliet's father
  • He is an enemy of Montague which causes the development of the plot in the story
  • He believes Juliet should be with Paris, not Romeo
  • Much like Tybalt he demands respect and gets very upset if it is not given
  • Lady Capulet
  • The mother of Juliet and wife of Capulet
  • She had Juliet when she was about fourteen years old and encourages Juliet to marry Paris
  • She requires the help of the Nurse to give her support in raising Juliet
  • Montague
  • Romeo's dad
  • Is Enemies with Capulet, Juliet's father
  • Lady Montague
  • Romeo's mother
  • She passes away after Romeo gets exiled from Verona of grief
  • Paris
  • Capulet's ideal husband for Juliet
  • Acts like he is already married to Juliet when Capulet promises him his daughter
  • Benvolio
  • Nephew of Montague and cousin and friend of Romeo
  • Typically has to settle situations to keep them from getting out of hand in public
  • Is accused of having a bad temper when alone
  • Helps Romeo stop thinking about Rosaline even when he's in love with Juliet
  • Prince Escalus
  • Prince of Verona
  • Mercutio and Paris' kinsman
  • Peace is his greatest goal to maintain in the city of Verona
  • Friar John
  • Franciscan friar
  • Accused of trying to tell Romeo of Juliet's false death by Friar Lawrence
  • Unfortunately he gets locked up and never gets the message to Romeo
  • Balthasar
  • Romeo's servant
  • Tells Romeo that Juliet has passed
  • Doesn't know that the death was fake
  • Sampson & Gregory
  • The Capulet family servants
  • They hate the Montague's
  • Abram
  • Montague's servant
  • Fights Sampson and Gregory at the beginning of the play
  • The Apothecary
  • Apothecary of Mantua
  • Will not sell Romeo poison
  • Peter
  • Illiterate servant of the Capulet's that takes the nurse to meet Romeo
  • Is in charge of inviting guests to the feast the Capulet's host
  • Rosaline
  • Never is shown onstage but is said to be beautiful by the other characters
  • Swears to live a life of chastity
  • The Chorus
  • Acts as the narrator for the plays plots and themes
